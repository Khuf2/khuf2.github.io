Engaging and clever cartoons that embrace the arbitrary, and actively prompt you to question their existence.
If you thrive on confusion and appreciate the random, I’m your guy. As a youthful and experimental online cartoonist, I’m a refreshing addition to your Instagram feed and a bold character designer for personalized logos, branding, and commissions.

I’m a mixed-media artist from the San Francisco Bay Area who designs outlandish cartoon characters that merge humor with the surreal. I strive to create stimulating and provocative artworks that can be enjoyed by any audience because of their playful form, but also retain the capacity for deeper themes and intrigue. I primarily want to catch your attention, and once you’re hooked you can continue to revel in the hectic visual components, or delve further into how a piece connects with your story.
Personality is very important to me when I draw. It’s undeniably a key facet of an art piece, which allows a work to radiate an emotion, energy, or attitude. Anyone viewing the artwork can then enter a dialogue with the piece, connecting their own experiences to the moods bottled into each one. From manic joy to debilitating stress, I hope you can find a drawing in my gallery that resonates with you.

As far as professional work, I have been featured in a local gallery in my hometown of Menlo Park, and have done several album covers and concert posters for local musicians in Menlo Park and Santa Cruz.
